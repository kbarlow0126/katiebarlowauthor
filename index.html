Featured Book

The Boy with Red Hair

A gentle story about fishing, friendship, patience, and discovering that sometimes the best part is the waiting.

Meet Cole, a curious boy with bright red hair, a love of fishing, and a gift for noticing the small wonders around him.

Set beside a peaceful pond in Stanley, Virginia, this heartwarming picture book celebrates quiet moments, unexpected friendships, and the joy of slowing down.

  • Perfect for children ages 4–8
  • A warm read-aloud for home, school, or library
  • Inspired by Virginia's Shenandoah Valley

Meet Katie

Stories inspired by home, family, and small-town wonder.

Katie Barlow is a children's author who creates warm, imaginative stories filled with friendship, curiosity, and adventure.

Her work is inspired by the beauty of Virginia's Shenandoah Valley, the town of Stanley, and the unforgettable moments that make childhood so special.

When she is not writing, Katie enjoys spending time with her boys, finding inspiration in everyday life, and dreaming up the next story.
